Saffron Beaches!

Saffron has many state owned beaches along our coast!

Our beaches are kept clean for our citizens and visiting people! On some days we do require beach tags. You must purchase these tags from the state and show them to our workers.

Saffron beaches are named after states in the Us. The following beaches are state-owned and can be used. Texas, Ohio, New York, New Jersey.
