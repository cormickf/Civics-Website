Saffron voting system

Election / Voting Information

Saffron is going to be running there elections in parts. The city will be broken into 3 districts.

One district will vote at a time. This is to keep the elections safe,organized,and fraudless.

In order to find out what district you are in or where you need to go to vote please visit the city hall.

Saffron's Party Policy

Saffron’s political rules state that our candidate will be nonpartisan.

This is so that our elections can be just for what the city needs not what a party needs

Also, this will help keep Saffron’s politics as clean as our beaches and city!

Type of Government

Saffrons Government is set up as a strong mayor plan.

This means our council has power but our mayor has the power to veto what ever they feel they must.

This is most closely related to the president and congress.

The council picked this because the council wants to run by them selves but,

also want some one to over see them and step in if they feel they are doing something wrong.

Our city council will have 7 members!
